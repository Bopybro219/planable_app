Cookies

Session and sign-in basics

Planira keeps this simple. Cookies are used for sign-in and session security, not for ads or tracking.

What Planira uses cookies for

Login and session state Planira currently uses one Flask session cookie to keep you signed in and connect requests to the right account.
Security for signed-in actions Planira also uses session-backed security tokens to help protect forms and account actions.
Turnstile anti-abuse checks Protected forms may load Cloudflare Turnstile to reduce spam and automated abuse. This is separate from advertising or analytics tracking.
Email preferences Newsletter opt-in and unsubscribe preferences are stored on the server, but Planira does not use cookies to track newsletter engagement.
Consent preferences Planira stores your cookie choices in a small consent cookie so the site can remember whether optional analytics is allowed on future visits.

What Planira does not use cookies for

No ads cookies Planira does not use advertising cookies.
Analytics only after consent If Google Analytics 4 is enabled in a live environment, it only loads after you opt in. Planira uses it for high-level product events and does not send personal data such as names, email addresses, support messages, or exact addresses.
No third-party tracking cookies Planira does not currently set third-party tracking cookies for marketing purposes, and Google Ads is not active.

Keeping this accurate

If Planira changes its analytics setup, adds marketing tools, or starts using more cookie-based features later, this page should be updated before those changes are treated as normal. Today, ads are not active, analytics stays off until consent is given, and transactional emails, support replies, and opt-in newsletters do not rely on ad-tracking cookies.
