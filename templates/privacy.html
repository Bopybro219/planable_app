Privacy

How Planira handles your data

A plain-English explanation of what Planira stores, why it is stored, and where the limits are.

What Planira collects

Account details Planira uses Google sign-in. When you log in, Planira stores your email address and may also store your name and profile picture from Google.
Search usage Planira stores search events so member limits, account summaries, and product review can work properly.
API access records When API access is used, Planira stores the API key record, a hash of the key rather than the raw key, and usage logs such as endpoint, status code, and when the key was used.
Community and staff records Planira stores comments, moderation decisions, staff audit logs, and internal venue workflow records so the service can be reviewed, improved, and protected from misuse.
Email records Planira can store support messages, newsletter consent state, and a limited record of which transactional emails were sent so duplicate payment or welcome emails can be avoided.
Anti-abuse checks Protected forms may use Cloudflare Turnstile to help distinguish real visitors from automated abuse. Planira verifies the Turnstile response on the server before accepting the submission.
Optional analytics If Google Analytics 4 is enabled for a live environment, Planira only loads it after you opt in to analytics cookies. It is used for high-level product signals such as search submissions, place views, pricing views, contact submissions, OAuth sign-ins, and API key creation.
Optional advertising If Google AdSense is enabled on supported public pages, Planira only loads it after you opt in to marketing cookies. Planira does not load advertising scripts on admin, staff, account, or similar internal pages.

What Planira uses that data for

Running your account Your email and session data keep you signed in and tie account activity to the right member profile.
Managing search and API limits Usage logs help Planira apply preview limits, track credits, and understand how the product is being used.
Moderation and abuse review Comments, API usage logs, staff audit records, and Turnstile verification outcomes help Planira review suspicious activity, moderation decisions, and operational changes.
Improving venue information Planira uses contribution and verification history to improve accessibility records and make the product more useful.
Transactional and support emails Planira may send welcome emails, account or payment updates, support replies, and staff notifications tied to messages you send through the contact form.
Newsletter opt-in Newsletter emails are only sent when you explicitly opt in. Every newsletter email includes an unsubscribe link, and opting out does not require logging in.
Minimal analytics only after consent If analytics is enabled, Planira uses it only after you consent and only for aggregated product understanding. Planira does not send names, email addresses, support message content, or exact addresses to Google Analytics.
Advertising only after consent If advertising is enabled, Planira only loads Google AdSense after you consent to marketing cookies. Google may use cookies or similar technologies for personalised or non-personalised ads depending on your settings, location, and Google’s own systems.

Payments

If paid plans or API packs are enabled, checkout is handled by Stripe. Planira stores the payment status and the account details needed to match a checkout to the right user. Planira does not store full card details on its own servers.

Data retention

Account data Planira keeps account data while your account is active. It can be deleted or anonymised after prolonged inactivity or if you ask for deletion.
Support messages Support messages are usually kept for up to 90 days so issues can be resolved properly, then deleted or anonymised.
Search activity Search activity is usually kept for up to 30 days for performance review, abuse prevention, and product improvement.
API usage logs API usage logs are usually kept for up to 30 days for rate limiting, debugging, and abuse prevention.
Newsletter data Newsletter signup and consent data is kept until you unsubscribe. After that, Planira may keep a minimal record so your opt-out can be respected.
Audit and security logs Audit and security logs are usually kept for up to 90 days so abuse reports, moderation decisions, and system issues can be reviewed.
Uploaded content Uploaded content such as place images and community comments is kept while it is relevant to the platform or until it is removed, rejected, or moderated out.

These retention periods are meant to be practical and proportionate for the current service. They may change as Planira evolves, especially if new safety, billing, or compliance needs appear.

If you want account or personal data deleted, you can request that through Planira support using the contact details shown on the site.

What Planira does not claim

Planira aims to be careful and clear, but this is still an early product. These pages describe the current system as it exists today. They are not meant to sound bigger or more polished than the implementation behind them. Planira does not load analytics or advertising before consent, does not place ads on internal staff or account pages, and does not send personal details like names, email addresses, support messages, or exact addresses to analytics tools. If the advertising setup changes later, these pages should be updated first. If you need help with an account or data question, use the contact details in the footer, including {{ contact_phone }}.
