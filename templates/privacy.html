Privacy

How Planira handles your data

Plain-English notes for a beta-stage product that tracks accounts, venue searches, and community contributions.

What we keep

Account detailsYour name, email, and profile image come from Google sign-in.
Search activityWe store search activity so usage limits, account summaries, and staff product review can work properly.
Community contributionsComments and moderation history are kept so venue information stays useful and safer to trust.

How it is used

Planira uses this data to run the product, support moderation, improve venue records, and understand what parts of the search experience need work. We do not aim to make the legal side feel intimidating, but we do want to be clear.
