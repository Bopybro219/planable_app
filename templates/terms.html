Terms

Using Planira fairly

Short, readable terms for an early product that mixes member search, accessibility data, and preview API access.

Using the service

Use your account honestly Do not impersonate someone else, share access in misleading ways, or try to bypass account or usage limits.
Use the API responsibly Planira’s API is an early access feature. Limits, pricing, and access rules may change as the product develops, uptime is not guaranteed, and you are responsible for keeping your API keys secure.
No misuse of staff or operational tools Internal tools, staff workflows, and operational endpoints are not public features and must not be accessed without permission.

Accessibility data disclaimer

Planira tries to make accessibility information more useful, but it is still best-effort information. A venue record may be incomplete, out of date, or wrong. People should still use their own judgement and, where needed, contact a venue directly before relying on the information.

Contributions and moderation

Contribute carefully Comments and accessibility updates should be honest, specific, and useful.
Planira may moderate content Planira may review, approve, reject, or remove contributions if they are inaccurate, abusive, misleading, or unsafe.
Misuse can lead to suspension Accounts or API keys may be limited, suspended, or revoked if Planira detects abuse, attempts to scrape around restrictions, or other misuse of the service.

Payments and preview features

Some paid plans or API packs may be offered through Stripe when enabled. Early pricing, plan shapes, and feature limits may change as Planira learns what is sustainable and useful.

Service maturity

Planira is still evolving. Features may change, be removed, or move behind different access rules. The goal is to be transparent about that rather than pretend the service is more settled than it is.
